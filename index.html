Little Red Riding Hood

Chapter 1

Once upon a time there lived in a certain village a little country girl. One day her mother, having made some cakes, said to her, "Go, my dear, and see how your grandmother is doing, for I hear she has been very ill. Take her a cake, and this little pot of butter.

Chapter 2

As Little Red Riding Hood was going through the woods, she met with a wolf, who had a very great mind to eat her up, but he dared not, because of some woodcutters working nearby in the forest. He asked her where she was going. The poor child, who did not know that it was dangerous to stay and talk to a wolf, told him that she was going to visit her ill granny, and give her a cake and a pot of butter.

Chapter 3

The wolf ran as fast as he could, taking the shortest path, and the girl took a roundabout way, entertaining herself by gathering nuts, running after butterflies, and gathering bouquets of little flowers. It was not long before the wolfarrived at the old woman's house. He knocked at the door and tricked the granny into thinking he was her daughter, who's bringing a cake and a pot of butter. When he entered at the old woman's house, he ate her up in an instant.

Chapter 4

Little Red Riding Hood arrived sometime later and knocked on the door. The wolf answered with a big voice, but the girl believed it was just a consecuence of the cold. She entered the house. The wolf, hiding under the blankets of the bed, told her to put the cake and the pot of butter on the table and to get in bed with her granny.

Chapter 5

Little Red Riding Hood got into bed.

"Grandmother, what big arms you have!"

"All the better to hug you with, my dear."

"Grandmother, what big legs you have!"

"All the better to run with, my child."

"Grandmother, what big ears you have!"

"All the better to hear with, my child."

"Grandmother, what big eyes you have!"

"All the better to see with, my child."

"Grandmother, what big teeth you have got!"

"All the better to eat you up with."

And after saying that words, the wolf ate Little Red Riding Hood.
